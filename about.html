Our Mission & Vision

At Abiding Place Fellowship, we believe the church should be a welcoming community where everyone belongs, grows in their faith, and experiences Christ's transforming love. Our mission is to create a place where families can find home, where seekers can find truth, and where believers can find fellowship.

We are dedicated to Bible-centered teaching, warm community, and practical application of God's Word to everyday life. Whether you've been a Christian for decades or are just beginning to explore faith, you'll find a place to belong here.

Our Statement of Faith

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God, fully authoritative for all matters of faith and practice. Every word is God-breathed and profitable for teaching, rebuking, correcting, and training in righteousness.

Jesus Christ

We believe in the full deity and humanity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death, bodily resurrection, ascension, and promised return. He is the only way to salvation and eternal life.

The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit convicts the world of sin, righteousness, and judgment. He regenerates believers, indwells them, seals them for redemption, and empowers them for Christian living and service.

The Church

We believe the church consists of all believers in Jesus Christ, united as His body. We are called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, evangelism, and serving others in love and humility.

Salvation

We believe salvation is a free gift of God received through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by works. All who believe are justified, adopted as children of God, and guaranteed eternal life.
