Global & Local Missions

"Therefore go and make disciples of all nations..." — Matthew 28:19-20

Where We Serve

Abiding Place Fellowship partners with ministries across the globe — praying, giving, and going where God leads.

Grenada 🇬🇩

Prayer & Praise Revival Ministries: Pastor Rev. Ashley & Carmen Roberts. Spice Worship Ministries: Pastor Alvin & Bernal Roberts.

Abiding Place comes alongside JJCM (Jehovah Jireh Christian Ministries), with whom Pastor Ashley is affiliated, to help with rebuilding the church building, ministering to their fellowship, and encouraging them in the things of the Lord.

Just as Abiding Place has been a blessing to Prayer & Praise Revival Ministries, so have they been a blessing to Abiding Place — through brotherly love and the preaching of God's Word when Pastor Ashley visits us at Abiding Place Fellowship.

"And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age." — Matthew 28:20

Haiti 🇭🇹

Partnering with Global Christian Ministry Forum (GCMF) to provide financial gifts to children in Haiti. Our heart is to help them overcome the many obstacles Haiti has faced over the years.

Our objective is to give children a safer and happier life with opportunities they would not have experienced without our help. Support provides schooling, health care, clothing, and food distributions.

Our goal: equip and prepare children to have a successful future — physically and spiritually — to impact and change Haiti for generations to come.

"Defend the weak and the fatherless; uphold the cause of the poor and the oppressed." — Psalm 82:3

Uganda 🇺🇬

King of Kings Ministries: Pastor Rev. Silas & Nicola in Mbarara, Uganda.

Abiding Place continually prays for all those in relationship with us. King of Kings Ministries is one ministry God has put on our hearts to lift up. Our Pastor and his wife Kate worked with Nicola in a ministry in Canada for several years, and still treasure their relationship.

We support them with prayer and financial support through a mutual friend to both ministries.

"Carry each other's burdens, and in this way you will fulfill the law of Christ." — Galatians 6:2

Unstoppable Blessing 🌍

Rev. Donna Parachin — a very special friend and mentor to Pastor Gord and Kate. They have worked together in several ministries over 25+ years (Ellel, Singing Waters Ministries).

Abiding Place counts it a blessing to partner with Rev. Donna in "Unstoppable Blessing" — she travels worldwide touching people with the incredible love of the Father: Asia, China, Singapore, Taiwan, Australia, Europe, Africa. In Canada she ministers to First Nations peoples (Cree, Ojibwa, Naskapi, Inuit in Nunavut).

When home, Rev. Donna blesses Abiding Place Fellowship with the tangible touch of Father God and teachings that encourage us to love all people like Jesus loves us.

"He has sent me to bind up the brokenhearted, to proclaim freedom for the captives..." — Isaiah 61:1
