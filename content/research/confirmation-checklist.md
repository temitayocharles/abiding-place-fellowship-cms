Complete this before connecting a final custom domain or announcing the site as official.

- Confirm the preferred public name: Abiding Place Fellowship or Abiding Place Ministries.
- Confirm Rev. Gord Horsley’s current title.
- Confirm Kate Horsley’s preferred title and biography.
- Approve a current leadership roster, roles and photos.
- Confirm the current affiliation and partner list.

## Weekly gatherings

- Confirm Sunday, Tuesday and Wednesday meeting times.
- Confirm the current Tuesday and Wednesday study topics.
- Confirm the exact venue room and Entrance Door “F”.

## Contact and safeguarding

- Confirm phone, cell and email.
- Confirm whether a postal code should be displayed.
- Approve a privacy notice and message-retention policy before enabling forms.
- Approve a child-photo and vulnerable-adult-photo policy.

## Media

- Confirm ownership or permission for each image selected from the legacy website.
- Obtain consent before reusing photographs of children, seniors, mission participants or third parties.
- Select a current logo file and current photographs of the congregation and leadership.
- Confirm whether any official Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, podcast or livestream account exists. Do not use the unrelated San Diego ministry at abidingplace.org.

## Events and giving

- Supply current confirmed events.
- Confirm whether online giving is offered and provide the approved destination.
- Do not publish a donation form or payment link until ownership and charity/receipt details are approved.
