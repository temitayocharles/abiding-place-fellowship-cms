# Official-media inventory

`media-manifest.json` contains the 84 unique image URLs exposed during the research sweep of the church’s legacy official website.

Publication on the old website does not prove unrestricted reuse rights. Before copying an asset into this repository, confirm church ownership, photographer permission and subject consent. Apply additional review to photographs involving children, seniors, veterans, mission participants and third parties.

The Sunday-service gallery could not be completely inventoried because the legacy host presented an anti-bot verification page.

Use `scripts/fetch-official-media.py` only after rights approval. It downloads approved manifest entries into `assets/official-media/` and does not alter HTML automatically.
