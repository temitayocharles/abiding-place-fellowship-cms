Our Statement of Faith

The carefully presented ten beliefs published by Abiding Place Fellowship.

"Now faith is assurance of things hoped for, a conviction of things not seen." — Hebrews 11:1

1

The Bible

We believe the Bible to be the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.

2

The Triune God

We believe there is one God, eternally manifested as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

3

Jesus Christ

We believe in the virgin birth of Jesus Christ, His atoning sacrifice through His shed blood, His bodily resurrection, His ascension, and His personal return in power and glory.

4

Salvation

We believe regeneration and conversion through faith in Jesus Christ are essential for the salvation of lost and sinful humanity.

5

Water Baptism

We believe water baptism is not a saving ordinance, but is an essential act of obedience to the Gospel and should be administered by immersion.

6

Life in the Holy Spirit

We believe the Gospel includes holiness of heart and life, healing of the body, and a personal experience with the Holy Spirit through which the gifts of the Spirit become active in believers of every age.

7

Resurrection & Judgment

We believe in the bodily resurrection of believers and in the final judgment of all humanity — the just into eternal life and the unjust into eternal damnation.

8

Unity of Believers

We believe in the spiritual unity of all believers in our Lord Jesus Christ.

9

Spiritual Gifts & Five-Fold Ministry

We believe in the gifts of the Holy Spirit and the five-fold ministry given to equip the saints for the work of the ministry, thereby edifying the body of Christ.

10

Marriage

We believe marriage is holy, ordained by God as a union of one man and one woman in covenant relationship.

Questions About Our Faith?

We welcome conversations about what we believe and why. Contact us to discuss membership, baptism, or any doctrinal questions.
