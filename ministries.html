Our Ministries

👶

Kids Ministry (Ages 3-12)

Fun, safe, and Bible-centered programs for children. Our volunteers create age-appropriate lessons that help kids understand God's love and grow in their faith. Available during Sunday services.

👦

Youth Ministry (Grades 6-12)

A dynamic environment where teens can ask questions, build authentic friendships, and discover their identity in Christ. Weekly gatherings, retreats, and service projects.

👩

Women's Ministry

Community, discipleship, and empowerment for women of all ages. Monthly gatherings, Bible studies, and service opportunities designed to encourage and uplift.

👨

Men's Ministry

Building brothers in Christ through fellowship, accountability, and spiritual growth. Monthly breakfasts, Bible studies, and outreach initiatives.

📖

Bible Study Groups

Small groups meet throughout the week for deeper study and fellowship. Currently studying Acts (Tue 10 AM) and Romans (Wed 7 PM) at Mel Lloyd Centre.

🤝

Outreach & Missions

Serving our community and beyond through local partnerships, mission trips, and charitable initiatives. Get involved in making a difference.
