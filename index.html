Step Inside Our Fellowship

Our worship space in the Chapel at the Mel Lloyd Centre provides a warm, welcoming atmosphere for gathering as the body of Christ. With comfortable seating, natural light, and a focus on Christ-centered worship, it's a place where you can encounter God's presence alongside fellow believers.

Whether you're joining us for Sunday worship, a midweek Bible study, or a special event, you'll find a community that values authentic relationships and biblical truth.

Rooted in Christ. Serving Shelburne.

Abiding Place began its Shelburne fellowship in December 2004 and continues to pursue an interactive, interdenominational expression of Christian community.

Since 2004

Established in Shelburne

The fellowship was formed to help people abide in Christ, grow in their gifts and experience healing, wholeness and sound Biblical teaching.

One body, many backgrounds

Interdenominational Fellowship

Members come from varied Christian traditions, united around relationship with Jesus Christ, the authority of Scripture and the work of Holy Spirit.

Local and global service

Community and Mission

The ministry has served seniors, veterans and local community initiatives while supporting Christian mission relationships in Grenada, Haiti, Uganda and beyond.
